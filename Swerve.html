Swerve Drivebase

At robotics we need to make our robot drive, and one of the best ways to do that is using something called swerve modules. These modules allow us to move at quick speeds and move from side to side without turning the whole robot. Although, the motors that are used on our swerve modules, the Falcon 500's, were discontinued, but we didn't want to buy new motors and different modules. The issue is the new motor we planned to use, the Kraken X60s were not compatible with our modules. I lead the project for figuring out how we ould do this. We had to do some problem solving, we had to make custom gears to put inside our modules to make it compatible. Now we have improved swerve modules and are able to go faster than ever before.
